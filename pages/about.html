About Green Earth Initiative

Green Earth Initiative (GEI) is an independent non-profit organisation focused on restoring habitats, reducing pollution and empowering communities through education and practical environmental programs.

Our Vision

A world where communities are resilient, nature is protected, and future generations inherit a healthy planet.

Our Mission

  • Restore degraded landscapes through community-led tree planting.
  • Reduce waste and increase recycling participation.
  • Protect water resources via conservation training and infrastructure support.
  • Provide environmental education to schools and local groups.

Our Team

GEI is run by a small team of environmental practitioners, community coordinators and volunteers. We partner with local leaders and experts to deliver lasting impact.

Impact to Date

  • 5,000+ trees planted across five provinces.
  • 12 community clean-ups resulting in over 3 tonnes of waste removed.
  • 50+ school workshops delivered reaching 4,000 students.
